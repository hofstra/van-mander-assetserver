Curing dogs of mange

Give them a pill of precipitate, that is to say iii or 4, or up to 5 grains, mixed with their meat. Having given it to them one one week, give them another seven or eight days after.

Excellent mustard

Dry bread in an oven, then lard it with cloves & cinnamon & thus put it to soak in good wine. Then, pass everything through a tammy cloth, being well pestled, & incorporate it with your mustard seed.
