Thus having placed your animal on the tourtelle shaped like a mandore, place all around a circle of the same earth, in this way & join everything well together in a circle, then, with a big knife, with which you have cut your tourtelle to the right size, pare the outside which exceeds it, & place there a piece to close it as you see in b.
