Petard

They are made better in crucible form, hollow at the narrower at the bottom & becoming larger towards the muzzle. It is necessary to pestle the powder thoroughly & put on top of it either sand or cork or better still a fitted plate of wax thoroughly pestled on top, for it seals precisely & makes for greater force. The ball only makes a hole, the powder alone makes more of an effect. One holds that, once loaded & kept in a dry place for fifteen days, they have more force.
