Lettering of gold on paper

Write with milk of the fig tree then let it dry a little, next lay the gold leaf, & once quite dry, rub with a linen cloth and only the lettering will remain.

For bursting grenades and giving force to fireworks

Put among the powder six times as much of quicksilver and the sixth part of quicksilver.

Tempering of balls for piercing armor of proof

Heat the lead ball as much as you can without melting it, and when it will be very hot, temper it in the strongest vinegar that you can. And do this four or five times. Next, chop some lard very small & some linen also very finely, mix all together & use it as wadding, and by a little force & very precisely, make it to go into the arquebus or pistol. And before firing, cha, if possible, make it so that the ball is hot.

For making a breach in a wall by night

Having made a hole in the wall as you know, put a petardinside, charged with powder, which should be as if lying down & the other upright, then plug the hole & give fire.
