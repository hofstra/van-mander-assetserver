Once very white from the force of being hot, I cast in it some pulverized calamine, raw & pure. I cast it in its very red mold. It came out very neat & thin like paper, & hollow on the reverse. But because it had developed a chappe, I reheated it, that is to say reddened, let it cool, then put it to be whitened, of one part raw tartar & of one half common salt.
