Weary horses

For fortifying a harried horse, they make it drink some of the aforesaid wine through a horn, and it finds itself disposed for doing an even greater labor.

Eau-de-vie

The Irish do not drink any wine because they convert it into eau-de-vie, which they use almost as habitually as we use wine.
