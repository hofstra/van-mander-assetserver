For making water for pain of the eyes

Take white copperas, & heat it a little on the fire, then put it as a powder on a white cloth stretched over a very clean glass, & the powder on top, then take fountain water & pass it through the cloth, while stirring the aforesaid powder with the finger until it has all gone through. Then wash your y eyes at any hour you wish & with the help of God, you will be very well.
