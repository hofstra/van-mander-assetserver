Aquafortis

If your retort is well luted one ought not to surround it with ashes, which would only get in the way of chasing the spirits properly. But when, at the end, you give it violent fire, surround it with coals which should be lit elsewhere beforehand, as those in the furnace should be, in order that they do not crackle & do not make smoke. Put the said lit coals around the body of the retort, & not close to the neck, which does not need to be heated as vigorously. The top grate, where you place your retort, is sufficiently far, one dour or half a foot, from the bottom grate where the charcoal is placed, for by doing this not as much of it is wasted.
