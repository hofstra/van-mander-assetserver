Because you always have to make the gate by the tail, and that it is so delicate and thin that the casting will find it hard metal would run with difficulty, especially when it is curled, roll wax in little threads of this thickness and apply some with the hot iron wire, as said, one at the end of the tail & the others, that are applied in the same way, from one edge side of the tail to the other, as you see depicted. But take heed to make sure, that with the end of the hot iron wire, the end of the wax barely touches the animal, for the sand of the second cast will not touch this part. But make sure that the end of the wax arranges itself only at the edge of the empty part of the first mold. Make also conduits of wax around the legs & around the contours of the body, which are a little long & they will serve as feeders for the molded thing.
