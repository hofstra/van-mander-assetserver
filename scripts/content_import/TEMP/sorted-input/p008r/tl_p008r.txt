<head>Lettering of <m>gold</m> on <m>paper</m></head>

<ab>Write with <m>milk of the <pa>fig tree</pa></m> then let it dry a
little, next lay the <m>gold leaf</m>, &amp; once quite dry, rub with a
<tl><m>linen</m> cloth</tl> and only the lettering will remain.</ab>

<head>Tempering of balls for piercing armor of
proof<comment>c_008r_02</comment></head>

<ab>Heat the <m>lead</m> ball as much as you can without melting it, and
when it will be very hot, temper<comment>c_008r_03</comment> it in the
<m>strongest vinegar</m> that you can. And do this four or five times.
Next, chop some <m>lard</m> very small &amp; some <m>linen</m> also very
finely, mix all together &amp; use it as wadding, and by a little force
&amp; very precisely, make it to go into the arquebus or pistol. And
before firing, <del><fr>cha</fr></del>, if possible, make it so that
the ball is hot.</ab>
